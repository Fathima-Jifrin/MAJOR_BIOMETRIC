About the Biometric Ballot System

The Election Commission of India (ECI) is committed to ensuring free and fair elections. The Biometric Ballot System (BBS) is a revolutionary step towards increasing voter security, reducing fraud, and promoting transparency in the electoral process.

With BBS, voter identity verification is conducted using biometric data, such as fingerprints or facial recognition, ensuring that only eligible voters can participate in elections. This reduces the risk of voter duplication and identity fraud.

  • Secure biometric authentication methods
  • Voter verification using government-issued biometric data
  • Integration with Aadhaar and other national ID databases
  • Improved transparency in voting records

Key Features of the Biometric Ballot System

Biometric Authentication

Accurately verifies voter identity using fingerprints, iris scans, or facial recognition technology, ensuring that only registered voters can cast their vote.

Fraud Prevention

Eliminates multiple voting and voter impersonation by securely verifying the identity of each voter before they can proceed.

Data Privacy

All biometric and personal data are encrypted and stored securely, adhering to strict privacy regulations, ensuring that voter information remains confidential.

Real-Time Monitoring

Election officials can monitor the voting process in real-time, with data analytics to track voting trends and ensure a smooth electoral process.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Biometric Ballot work?

Voters authenticate their identity using biometric data at the polling station. Once authenticated, they can cast their vote electronically. This ensures that each person can vote only once.

Is my biometric data secure?

Yes, all biometric data is encrypted and stored securely according to global data protection standards. No unauthorized access to your data is allowed.

What if my biometric data doesn't match?

If there's a mismatch in biometric data, election officials will provide alternative ways to verify your identity to ensure your right to vote is not compromised.
